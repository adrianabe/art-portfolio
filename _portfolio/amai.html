Amai Surprise is a cute snack brand that sells healthy, dried fruit snacks for kids. Candy brands generally use bright and popping colors to attract kids into buying their products, so why not apply the same concept to real food?

Amai Surprise makes the packaging of fruit appealing, so kids can make a healthier choice when selecting food off the shelves. With the growing interest in Anime in the West, these magical girl mascots help sell the brand with their own unique look and accessories.
