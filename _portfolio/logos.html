DivvyUp

Did you know that over 60% of touring professional golfers try fixing their ball mark using a 3-inch tee? But tees are for keeping a golf ball stationary, not for repairing divots. A Divvy-Up is the correct and ideal tool to use for properly and cleanly fixing ball marks.
