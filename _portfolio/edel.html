Edel is a sparkling apple drink that encapsulates the aesthetics surrounding wine for those who don’t drink alcohol. Each individual bottle carries its own unique story of a mythical creature of antiquity with illustrations from the Nuremberg Chronicle.




PERSONA


Edel’s persona is a 25 year old male who’s a big fan of old architecture and art and loves hanging out with his friends at parties, eating at fancy restaurants when he can, and chilling with some good, retro music. He is a person who doesn’t drink alcohol either for personal or medical reasons, but still enjoys the aesthetic and wants to fit in.




MOODBOARD


Edel’s moodboard consists of Art Nouveau designs and fifteenth-century artwork with an emphasis on the Nuremburg Chronicle: a book consisting of all the cumulative knowledge people in Nuremburg, Germany believed about the world at the time.




IDEATION


Early sketches of the Edel logo and bottle design. I used my calligraphy skills to make the wordmark which I then scanned and printed to continue to add extra design elements by splashing ink or creating swirls.




THE BOTTLE


The front of the bottle consists of a laser-cut label with a trippy inner design of tumbling buildings that play off of the idea of distortion caused by alcohol.


The back of the bottle features a monster from the Nuremberg Chronicle. I researched each individual creature, while also creating some of their back-stories. I colorized some of the black and white images using photoshop. The type I used is Coquette for a vintage but playful look.




THE PACK


The outer packaging continues to play off of the Art Nouveau designs with the swirls and circles encapsulating all the strange creatures.




MYTHICAL MONSTERS PAMPHLET


Because there are only four bottles, I made sure to include a mini pamphlet with all the creatures which would be placed inside the 4-pack of bottles.




THE FAMILY

A work of art on the box and every bottle, Edel packaging design provides the consumer with an engaging story behind every creature and also the brand itself. Any adult can walk into a party with an Edel 4-pack and also entertain guests with the beauty of the design.
