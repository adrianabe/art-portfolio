Spectrum Studio creates vibrant imagery filled with drama, flair, and eye-catching appeal. I was brought into the process to redesign Spectrum Studio and Spectrum Animation's branding and website into a sleeker, eye-catching, and moderized look.






SPECTRUM STUDIO INTO SPECTRUM ANIMATION'S LOGO

Spectrum Animation's new logo acquired slight changes, with a twirling 3d effect and an eye-catching red-orange to differentiate itself from its parent Spectrum Studio. With Spectrum Studio existing for over 50 years, these small changes were made to indicate a modernized rebranding while also keeping its original personality in-tact.





SPECTRUM ANIMATION PAGE BEFORE AND AFTER

In this new website, the amount of projects were cut down and spread out to make the site looks cleaner. A banner video and animated thumbnails were created to emphasize the animation aspect of the website. Hovering over the logo would reveal a gif of the logo sketching itself.
