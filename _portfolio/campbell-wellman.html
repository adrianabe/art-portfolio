This real estate company was born out of an idea to provide more of a detailed and bottoms up approach to buying and selling real estate. Compromised of photos of beautiful, high-end homes and elegant typographic and design treatments, their branding communicates its promise of providing clients with excellent communication and luxurious California properties. I created advertisements, business cards, and helped design a brochure alongside another designer.
